places must visit

REWALSER

i had visited in dec 2018

Rewalsar or Tso Pema in Tibetan is a small town and a pilgrimage place in a nagar panchayat in Mandi district in India. It is located in the state of Himachal Pradesh. The local name for Rewalsar is Tri Sangam. Rewalsar Lake is a tourist spot in the area. Rewalsar Lake is a mid-altitude lake located on a mountain spur in the Mandi district, 22.5 km south-west from Mandi, in India. Its elevation is about 1,360 m ,This lake is surrounded by rugged mountains on all sides.



ELANTE MALL-CHANDIGARH

i had visited in dec 2018

Elante Mall is a shopping mall in the city of Chandigarh in India. With the gross leasable area of 1,000,000 sq ft, it is the ten largest shopping mall in Northern India and the seventh largest in India. Elante is spread over an area of 20 acres. It is part of a mixed use real estate development project by Larsen & Toubro called Elante.



LAKSHADWDEEP

i had visited in dec 2018

Lakshadweep Islands are also known as the Laccadive Islands. It is a group of islands in the Laccadive Sea. This island is about 220 to 440 km off the coast of the South West Indian state of Kerala. Of the 36 islands covering an area of 32 sq km only 11 islands are inhabited. This ship takes you to the islands of Lakshadweep through the Arabian Sea. It includes a five-day coral reef package cruise to visit the islands of Minicoy, Kadmat, Kavaratti, and Kalpeni. Apart from meals and refreshments, diving, snorkelling, swimming and entertainment programmes are a part of the package.
